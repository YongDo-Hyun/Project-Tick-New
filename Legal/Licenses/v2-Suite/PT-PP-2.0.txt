1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Project Tick (“Project Tick”, “we”,
“us”) collects, uses, and protects information when you access or use
the Project Tick website and related online services (the “Services”).

We are committed to respecting user privacy and limiting data
collection to what is necessary.

This Privacy Policy applies only to website and online services
operated by Project Tick.

It does not apply to:
software distributed under open-source licenses;
proprietary or enterprise software governed by a separate EULA;
third-party websites or services.

3. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the Services, we may collect:

a) Information You Provide
account information (such as username or email address);
form submissions, feedback, or support requests;
content voluntarily submitted by you.

b) Technical and Usage Information
IP address;
browser type and version;
operating system;
approximate location (country or region);
access timestamps and pages visited.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data.

4. Purpose of Data Collection

We collect information solely for the following purposes:
operating and maintaining the Services;
account management and authentication;
security, abuse prevention, and system integrity;
responding to inquiries or support requests;
legal and compliance obligations.

We do not sell personal data.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

The Services may use cookies or similar technologies strictly for:
session management;
authentication;
security-related functions.

We do not use cookies for advertising or cross-site tracking.

We do not share personal data with third parties except:
where required by law or legal process;
where necessary to protect the rights, security, or integrity of

operation.

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the
purposes described in this Policy or to comply with legal obligations.

When no longer required, data is securely deleted or anonymized.
